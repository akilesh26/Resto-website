About Us

We take pride in our warm, welcoming atmosphere where every guest feels like family.

At Rustic Stove, we bring you the vibrant and diverse flavors of South India right to your table. Nestled in the heart of Bengaluru, our cozy eatery offers a charming escape where you can savor authentic South Indian dishes crafted with passion and tradition.

Our menu is a celebration of the rich culinary heritage of South India, featuring a delightful array of dosas, idlis, vadas, and uttapams, all prepared from scratch using the freshest ingredients.
